Biography

I am a Ph.D. Candidate in the ECE Department at UC San Diego. I work at Existential Robotics Laboratory, advised by Prof. Nikolay Atanasov.
I received my M.S. degree from School of Electrical, Computer and Energy Engineering, Arizona State University in 2016. I received my B.Eng. degree from School of Astronatutics, Northwestern Polytechnical University in 2013.
